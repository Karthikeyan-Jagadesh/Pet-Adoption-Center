Transparent process

How adoption works

We've designed a straightforward journey so you always know what to expect—from your first browse to bringing your pet home.

1

Browse available pets

Visit our pets page to explore profiles, photos, and temperament tags. Use filters to narrow by species, age, and size.

2

Submit your application

Fill out the adoption form with your details and tell us why you're a great match. Selected pet names auto-fill from listings.

3

Meet & greet

Our team schedules a visit so you can interact with the pet and ask questions in person before making a decision.

Timeline

What to expect after you apply

Day 1

Application received

You get an on-screen confirmation immediately. Our coordinators begin reviewing your submission the same day.

Day 2–3

Initial review & call

We contact you to discuss your lifestyle, home environment, and answer any questions about the pet.

Week 1

Meet-and-greet visit

Visit the center, spend time with your potential companion, and meet our care team.

Week 2

Welcome home

Once approved, complete final paperwork and take your new family member home with post-adoption support.

Requirements

Before you apply

Stable home

A safe living environment with enough space for the pet's size and energy level.

Time & commitment

Willingness to provide daily care, exercise, and long-term companionship.

Open communication

Honest answers on the application help us make the best match for everyone.
