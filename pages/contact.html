FAQ

How long does adoption take?

Most adoptions are completed within 1–2 weeks, depending on scheduling and application review.

Can I visit before applying?

Yes. You're welcome to visit during open hours to meet pets and speak with our team.

Is there an adoption fee?

A modest fee covers vaccinations, spay/neuter, and basic medical care. We'll share details during your visit.
